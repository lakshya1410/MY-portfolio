  1. Define ICP: Input target criteria like Industry, Job Title, Tech Stack, and Budget.
  2. AI Search: The system utilizes Bright Data's infrastructure to scrape and aggregate data from LinkedIn and company websites.
  3. Analysis: OpenAI GPT-4o analyzes the gathered data against your ICP, assigning a confidence score (0-100%).
  4. Results: View detailed lead cards with contact info, company details, and actionable AI insights.
